 # Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Project - Superstore Dataset

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Superstore dataset to uncover meaningful business insights, identify sales trends, analyze customer behavior, and understand factors affecting profit and sales.

The analysis includes statistical summaries, data visualization, and interpretation of key findings using Python.

---

## Dataset

- Dataset: Superstore Dataset
- Format: CSV
- Domain: Retail Sales

---

## Objectives

- Explore and understand the dataset
- Perform statistical analysis
- Identify trends and patterns
- Analyze sales and profit across different categories
- Discover relationships between variables
- Present business insights through visualizations

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis Performed

- Data Loading
- Dataset Overview
- Statistical Summary
- Missing Value Analysis
- Duplicate Record Check
- Data Cleaning
- Correlation Analysis

---

## Visualizations

- Total Sales by Category
- Total Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Ship Mode Distribution
- Correlation Heatmap
- Sales Distribution Histogram

---

## Key Business Insights

- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- Sales and profits vary significantly across product categories.
- Some regions contribute more to overall sales and profitability.
- Consumer segment accounts for a large share of sales.
- Sales distribution indicates the presence of high-value transactions.
- Correlation analysis helps identify relationships among numerical variables.

---

## Conclusion

This Exploratory Data Analysis project demonstrates the process of understanding retail business data through statistical analysis and visualization. The insights obtained can support data-driven business decisions related to sales strategy, customer segmentation, and profitability.
